About Company

Chemlox Organics Private Limited is a leading agricultural solutions provider based in Telangana, India. Established with a vision to revolutionize farming practices through innovative products, we have been serving farmers across the country with high-quality agricultural inputs.

At Chemlox Organics Private Limited, we understand the challenges faced by modern farmers. Our team of agricultural experts and scientists work tirelessly to develop products that address specific crop needs, enhance soil health, and maximize yield while maintaining environmental sustainability.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Pedda Amberpet is equipped with the latest technology to ensure consistent quality and efficacy of our products. We adhere to strict quality control measures at every stage of production, from raw material selection to packaging and distribution.

With a strong distribution network across multiple states, we ensure that our innovative agricultural solutions reach farmers when they need them the most.

Mission

To empower farmers with innovative, sustainable, and effective agricultural solutions that enhance crop productivity, improve soil health, and contribute to food security.

We are committed to:

  • Developing high-quality agricultural inputs that address specific crop needs
  • Promoting sustainable farming practices that preserve natural resources
  • Providing technical support and knowledge to farmers
  • Contributing to the economic well-being of farming communities
  • Continuously innovating to meet evolving agricultural challenges

Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred agricultural solutions provider, recognized for innovation, quality, and commitment to sustainable agriculture.

We aspire to:

  • Lead the agricultural inputs industry through continuous innovation
  • Set new standards for product quality and efficacy
  • Expand our reach to serve farmers across the country and beyond
  • Contribute significantly to sustainable agricultural practices
  • Be recognized as a company that cares for farmers, communities, and the environment
